# FEDERAL JURISDICTION (MONTANA COMPONENT) {#federal-jurisdiction-montana-component .unnumbered}

## RICO Violations (18 U.S.C. § 1962) {#rico-violations-18-u.s.c.-1962 .unnumbered}

**Enterprise:** YWCA Missoula (nonprofit corporation), Missoula Police Department, and Missoula County Attorney's Office operating together as an association-in-fact enterprise.

**Pattern of Racketeering Activity:** 20+ predicate acts documented across 2012--2025, including:

- **Mail/Wire Fraud (18 U.S.C. § 1341, § 1343):** Federal and state grant applications and sponsor reports containing material misrepresentations regarding services, outcomes, staffing, and client protections.

- **Obstruction of Justice (18 U.S.C. § 1503, § 1512):** Suppression of complaints, retaliation against whistleblowers and complainants, and interference with reporting to law enforcement and regulators.

- **HIPAA Criminal Violations (42 U.S.C. § 1320d-6):** Systematic disclosure of protected health information by YWCA Missoula staff, including sensitive counseling information shared with abusers.

- **Witness Tampering (18 U.S.C. § 1512):** Intimidation and retaliation against individuals who complained about YWCA Missoula or reported misconduct by affiliated officers and prosecutors.

**Interstate Commerce Impact:** Federal grant funds, interstate communications with Washington-based co-conspirators, and use of electronic platforms to coordinate false reports and case strategies.

## Civil Rights Violations (18 U.S.C. § 241, § 242; 42 U.S.C. § 1983, § 1985) {#civil-rights-violations-18-u.s.c.-241-242-42-u.s.c.-1983-1985 .unnumbered}

- **Fourth Amendment:** Warrantless home entries, unreasonable searches and seizures, and false imprisonment directed at Mr. Nuno and others in Missoula.

- **First Amendment:** Retaliation following Mr. Nuno's formal YWCA complaint and subsequent criticism of institutional misconduct.

- **Fourteenth Amendment:** Malicious prosecution, denial of due process, and discriminatory treatment in charging and bail decisions.

- **Civil Rights Conspiracy:** Coordinated actions by YWCA Missoula staff, MPD officers, and local prosecutors to deprive individuals of equal protection and to silence critics.
