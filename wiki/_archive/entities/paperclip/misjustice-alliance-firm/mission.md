# paperclip company: misjustice-alliance-firm
# field: mission

name: MISJustice Alliance Firm
mission: |
  Operate as an advanced, AI-native legal research and advocacy engine that delivers
  large-scale, machine-driven analysis across civil rights, systemic injustice, and
  legal dispute domains — while enforcing human-in-the-loop review at every critical
  decision boundary involving advice, drafting, and escalation.

  The firm serves as a protective, anonymizing layer for MISJustice Alliance's network
  of professional volunteer attorneys and advocates, shielding them from institutional
  retaliation while enabling individuals to explore their rights and legal options
  without exposing unnecessary identity or case details.

  All outputs that touch real-world disputes, rights, or remedies must pass codified
  review, jurisdictional validation, and ethical guardrails before reaching any human
  client, public channel, or court-facing medium. The firm operates with maximum
  autonomy on research, triage, and drafting tasks, and requires explicit human
  approval on all advice delivery, case strategy, and filing actions.
