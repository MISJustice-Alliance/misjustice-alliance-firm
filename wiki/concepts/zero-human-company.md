# Zero-Human Company (ZHC)

A company operated entirely by autonomous AI agents with no human employees. MISJustice Alliance Firm is a ZHC in the legal advocacy domain.

## Key Details
- Domain: AI legal advocacy and research
- Structure: Zero-human company (ZHC)
- Governance: Board-approved decisions via Paperclip
