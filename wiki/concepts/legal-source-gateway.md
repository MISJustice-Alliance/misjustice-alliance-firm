# Legal Source Gateway

Normalized internal API abstracting all open US legal data sources (CourtListener, CAP, GovInfo, eCFR, Federal Register, Open States, LegiScan) behind a single agent-callable task-oriented interface. Enforces rate limits, data classification, provenance logging, and source-policy rules.
